# What is Leveling?

Leveling is the 'documentation' for working out. 

'Leveling' comes from the idea that muscle growth and body recomposition shares many parallels with 'leveling up' in a game. In truth, losing weight, gaining muscle, or doing both, simply requires sustained efforts in basic things over time.
